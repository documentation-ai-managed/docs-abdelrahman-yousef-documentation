## Set Up Your Account

Begin by creating your account to access all features.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Sign Up" icon="user-plus" title-type="p">
    Visit the sign-up page and enter your email, name, and password.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Verify Email" icon="mail" title-type="p">
    Check your inbox for the verification link and click it to activate.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Log In" icon="log-in" title-type="p">
    Return to the login page and sign in with your credentials.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout kind="tip" collapsed="false">
  Enable two-factor authentication in your profile settings for added security.
</Callout>

## Create Your First Project

After logging in, create a documentation project for your content.

<Steps>
  <Step title="New Project" icon="plus" title-type="p">
    Click the `New Project` button on the dashboard.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Configure" icon="settings" title-type="p">
    Enter a project name like `My API Docs`, select `Documentation` template, and choose your brand color `#3B82F6`.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Publish" icon="globe" title-type="p">
    Save and publish. Your site deploys automatically.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Create Initial Content

Add your first page using MDX. Start with frontmatter and structured content.

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="index.mdx" icon="file-text">

    description: Introduction to your docs.
    ---
